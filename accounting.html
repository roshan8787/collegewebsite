Why Study Accounting and Finance?

Accounting and finance are the backbone of every successful business. Learn how to manage budgets, track expenses, and understand key financial concepts that will prepare you for a career in the corporate world or help you manage your personal finances.

Financial Tips for Beginners

  • Always keep track of your expenses.
  • Learn the basics of budgeting and saving.
  • Understand the concept of compound interest.
  • Invest wisely and start early.
  • Build an emergency fund for unforeseen situations.
